What Our Website Does

  • Plant Species Identification: Instantly recognize plants through uploaded photos or names and get customized care instructions.
  • Customized Watering and Care Reminders: Receive personalized schedules based on your plant's needs and your local environment.

At PlantCare Assistant, we believe that taking care of plants should be a joyful, stress-free experience.
